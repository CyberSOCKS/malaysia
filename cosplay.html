Cosplay Competition

INTRODUCTION COSPLAY

Cosplay event has become a fan of cartoons and comics craze in Malaysia. From year to year, this cosplay culture grows. Generally, cosplay in Malaysia is divided into two, namely eastern @ cosplay anime (Japanese) and western cosplay. According to current trends, cosplay japanese pervading Malaysia arena.

Cosplay competition will not only promote the appearance Japanese cosplay and Western cosplay but also warmly welcomed participation of local character / animation malaysia. No discrimination of cosplay genre selected. The competition will outline 3 ratings, namely Craftsmanship (40%), Fidelity (40%), Performance (20%).

TERMS AND CONDITIONS

  • Open to all students and public.
  • Registration fee is RM 20.
  • Appearance character which is immoral and indecent is PROHIBITED.
  • Participants will be judged on craftmanship (Quality costume), Fidelity (Costume similarity with the original character) and Performance (Show). Bonus score and accessories to costume-made. Craftsmanship (40%), Fidelity (40%), Performance (20%).
  • Participants need to bring a picture character A4 size (if any) to be given to the referee to compare and song in MP3 form for cosplay skits.
  • Cosplayer need to create a showcase or skits on stage for the evaluation of the judges. Duration skits in 3-5 minutes. Skits will take place on the Saturday at 2.30 PM.
  • Only 3 of the best cosplayers will be selected.
  • The judges' decision is final.
  • The organizer reserves the right to reject any entry that violates any of the conditionswhich is specified.
  • The organizer reserves the right to use photos and video participants for promotional purposes.
  • Cosplay participants should always follow instructions from time to time from the secretariat program.
